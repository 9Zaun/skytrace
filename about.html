SkyTrace — Live Flight Intelligence

WHAT IS SKYTRACE

SkyTrace is a readability-first flight operations platform. Built to present live aviation data through a minimal, editorial interface — designed for clarity over decoration.

Departures, arrivals, gate activity, and airport status boards are organised as operational dashboards, not interactive maps or cluttered widgets.

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

The interface draws from airport FIDS systems and aviation instrument design. Every element is functional. Every line has purpose.

Typography is primary. Spacing is structural. The champagne-gold accent marks active states. Thin geometry provides context without decoration.
